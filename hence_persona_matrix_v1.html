Key discriminating dimensions: The sharpest separator between target and non-target isn't income or financial literacy — it's the combination of will to act (Sandra fails this) + unsatisfied by existing tools (Trevor fails this) + orientation toward structural health over performance (Jordan fails this). All three targets share the same gap: they know something is off but lack the clarity to address it. Priya and Dani differ in how they've tried to solve it — Priya through anxious accumulation of data, Dani through serial novelty-seeking — but both arrive at Hence with the same unmet need. The acquisition path differs (Priya needs trust; Dani needs a hook), but the product response is the same.
