Key assumptions
📈
All costs inflate at 2.5% / year
Your dream home, car, travel, and dining costs are expressed in today's dollars. Hence inflates every line item forward to your retirement date. A $500 flight today costs ~$830 in 2063 at 2.5%/yr. The math works in both directions — your savings also grow.
🏛
Social Security includes a 20% benefit haircut
Current projections suggest the SS trust fund faces pressure around 2033. Hence conservatively assumes benefits come in at ~80% of the current schedule. Your estimated $2,300/mo therefore reflects $1,840/mo in the plan. Toggle SS off to see the worst case.
